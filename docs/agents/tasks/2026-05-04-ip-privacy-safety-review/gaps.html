Gaps

Needs Future Review

  • Qualified legal review is still needed before shipping any starter responsibility library, category taxonomy, assessment, workbook-like flow, card-inspired UI, or educational curriculum.
  • V1 product architecture still needs to decide whether starter responsibilities ship at all. A custom-only inventory is lower risk.
  • The exact authentication implementation has not been selected. Future agents must document the chosen password hash, session storage, cookie settings, and logout/session expiration behavior.
  • Abuse and coercive-control safety language needs specialist review before public launch, especially for onboarding, partner invites, concern boards, and check-ins.
  • Export, deletion, household separation, and access revocation requirements need fuller product/legal review before real user data is stored.
  • No accessibility or localization review has been done for safety notices, privacy labels, or confirmation dialogs.

Open Product Questions

  • Whether two personas are enough for v1, or whether broader households need more member profiles.
  • Whether private notes should sync across devices or stay device/session scoped until a stronger account model exists.
  • How long audit/history events should be retained after a household deletes or archives a responsibility.
  • Whether any metrics should be visible before both personas have opted into shared review.
  • Whether future paid features change privacy, export, or deletion obligations.
